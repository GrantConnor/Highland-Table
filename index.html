About Us

Built for gathering, grounded in flavor.

Highland Table is a California restaurant inspired by the comfort of a well-set table and the energy of a lively open kitchen. Our dining room blends polished hospitality with an easygoing pace, making it just as fitting for date nights and celebrations as it is for casual dinners with friends.

Our approach centers on honest ingredients, strong technique, and dishes that feel generous without being fussy. From crisp starters and house-made pastas to grilled entrées, wood-fired pizzas, and indulgent desserts, every plate is designed to feel familiar, refined, and deeply satisfying.

We believe restaurants should feel memorable for the right reasons: warm service, thoughtful details, and food that brings people back. At Highland Table, every visit is meant to feel welcoming from the first pour to the last bite.
